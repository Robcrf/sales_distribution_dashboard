## üìå Overview

The **Sales Distribution Dashboard** provides a detailed view of sales performance across **regions, products, customers, and time periods**, with a strong focus on **distribution analysis and target tracking**.

This dashboard enables business users to monitor **total sales**, compare **actual sales vs targets**, analyze **regional performance**, identify **top customers and products**, and explore **geographical sales distribution** through an interactive map.

It also includes **monthly and weekly trends**, region and month slicers, and a **Light/Dark mode toggle** to improve usability and presentation.

## ‚ùì Key Business Questions Answered

### 1. What is the total sales performance?
- Displays overall **Total Sales** as a high-level KPI for quick performance assessment.

### 2. How do actual sales compare against targets over time?
- The **Monthly (Actual vs Target)** chart highlights performance gaps, overachievement, and underperformance by month.

### 3. Which regions contribute the most to total sales?
- The **Region-wise distribution** shows sales contribution by region, including export markets.

### 4. Which products and customers drive the highest sales?
- **Product-wise ranking** identifies top-selling products.
- **Top 3 Customers** highlights the most valuable customers by sales value.

### 5. How are sales distributed geographically?
- The **map visualization** provides insight into global sales presence and regional concentration.
